About me!

I was born in Bar, Montenegro. My nationality is Albanian. I spent my childhood in Ulcinj-Montenegro, enjoying the nice weather and beaches in summer. Then (age 10) I moved to Kosovo . I finished my education and got my highschool diploma there. Right after that (on 2015), my family decided to cross the Atlantic Ocean and that's when I moved to USA with them.
